YouTube wasn't built for listening

You opened YouTube for background audio. Two hours later, you're watching a documentary about a 1997 bank heist. The algorithm won.

Aviram Radio extracts pure audio from YouTube's attention machinery. No visual cortex hijacking. No recommendation rabbit holes. No completion percentages optimizing for "engagement."

  • Recommendation sidebars designed to break your focus
  • Autoplay queues that drift into uncharted territory
  • Video thumbnails competing for your attention
  • Usage patterns harvested and monetized

Field equipment specifications

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Mark Signal

Timestamp moments that matter. Stars cluster into peaks — your personal highlight map.

Peak Detection

Algorithm identifies high-density star regions. Navigate between peaks with bracket keys.

Smart Categories

Heavy Rotation. Deep Listens. Current Vibe. Your behavior surfaces naturally.

AI Archivist

Brief context notes via OpenAI or Anthropic. The detail that makes you hear it differently.

Retro Monitor

Draggable CRT overlay with scanlines. Syncs with main player. See the static.

Zero Backend

All data in localStorage. No accounts. No tracking. Clone and run.
